EYEMC

If you are here and reading this, I appreciate you taking the time to check out my new album. I have been working very hard to create an entire package around this project, including the music, music videos, promo material, and merch.

I’ve always struggled with promoting myself, and last year randomly lost access to my FB and IG pages, which pretty much destroyed my reach. To combat this, I’ve worked hard to create an aesthetic around this project, and make the promotion material art in itself. My goal is for this whole project, top to bottom, to be an audio visual experience for the viewer and my hope is to generate real interest in this album and my project as I continue to push myself artistically, and work to grow my audience. Any help in promotion, write-ups, features, etc. is very much appreciated and will go a long way in helping me spread my art!
